## 预热

如果想表达**更好地同国外的朋友交流**：
可以选择：
I want to speak English well. 
I want to speak with foreigners.

## 正式课程：

**序数词的读法**：
fifteen是升调，fifty是降调。

## 对话：

#### Introduce your family:

Lily is daughter.
Lily is Paul's daughter.  口语化表达：daughter = little girl  son = little boy
Paul is Lily's father. 口语化表达：father = dad = papa = daddy

Joe looks like Paul's father. 
looks like 更多的是描述外貌。
seems like 更多是行为气质像，这个句子用 seems like 更符合含义。

I think Musa is Joe's father.
如果表达不太确定的时候，可以使用 I guess... / My guess is that ...

Joe is 55 years old.
Joe is about 52 years old.
Joe is in his fifties.
Joe is in his early fifties.
Joe is in his mid-fifties.
Joe is in his late fifties.
Joe was born in the 70s.

I guess he was born in the 70s.
I believe he was born in 1975.

I guess Paul is 32 years old.

Lily seems like 7-year-old.
Lily seems like she is 7 years old.
Lily seems 7 years old / 7.

I think Musa is younger than Joe, I guess he is 46 years old.

I want to give you some information about my family.
My father's name is John, he is 54 years old.
My mother is a teacher, she is about 50 years old .
My sister is also a teacher, she is 27 years old. / My sister is a teacher too.

She is older than me, about 1 year old. （❌）
She is one year older than me.

#### Introduce your new colleague
font desk = reception 前台

A: Thanks for showing me the office.
B: You are welcome. / It's me honor. / My pleasure. /  I am happy to help.

There is an elevator over here.

A: It's such a big office.
B: Yeah, we have so many desks and computers here. / Yeah, it's quite a large office.

Yes, just one. / only one.

It's near/close to the manager's office.

**可以尝试使用问题中的部分关键词汇来回答问题。**

It's behind the manager's office.
The manager's office is in front of the restroom.

Sorry, execuse me ?
Sorry, pardon?
Sorry, please say it again.

The area is the kitchen. We usually eat food, drink coffee and have a rest.
Here is the kitchen.

Yes, you can use coffee machine to make coffee.

I do have my own coffee cup.
There are many coffee cups prepared for you. 这里使用 prepared for 有点生疏，一般用于和大人物聊天。
可以替换：There are many coffee cups over there.

and so on
and things like that 诸如此类。
